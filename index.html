Our Story

Founded in 2010, Cozy Corner Café began with a simple mission: to create a warm, welcoming space where community members could enjoy exceptional coffee and homemade treats.

We source our coffee beans from ethical, sustainable farms and roast them in small batches to ensure the freshest flavor. Our pastries are baked daily using traditional recipes and premium ingredients.

Shekinah Jabez Florentino

Founder & Head Barista
